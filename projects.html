Oil & Gas R&D

As a researcher in oil and gas, I leveraged data analysis and software development to improve production efficiency. My work also involved simulating surface facilities using physics-based models and mathematical calculations implemented in Python to optimize oil-water separation processes and sand control strategies.

Key Achievements

  • Created simulation models for surface facility designs, improving production efficiency and equipment performance.
  • Collaborated with industry leaders like OMV and Swarco to implement data-driven innovations in gravel packing systems.
  • Published research on hydrophobic coatings, integrating data analytics to validate experimental results.
